About CIIVES

Complete vascular care under one roof

  • NABH-accredited hospital ensuring the highest quality and safety standards
  • Dedicated operation theatre & fully functional Cath Lab
  • State-of-the-art facility – Laser, advanced Doppler machine, intravenous ultrasound, etc.
  • Both open & endovascular procedures by vascular super-specialists
  • A dedicated Diabetic Foot Care Clinic (DFCC)
  • Ethical & transparent care – no unnecessary tests or surgeries
  • Day-care procedures – return home the same day for many treatments
  • Timely intervention for emergency care
